The Founder

Jenil Chawda

Before founding Jenvor Studio, Jenil recognized a distinct gap in the luxury market: traditional production houses treated diamonds and precious metals like any other product. They lacked the specialized lighting techniques and deep understanding required to capture internal refraction and microscopic craftsmanship.

Jenvor was born from a singular obsession: to create a dedicated space where jewelry isn't just photographed, but directed. A place where every facet is illuminated with intent.

Today, the studio operates as a private atelier, partnering intimately with select brands to elevate their digital presence through narrative-driven motion content.
